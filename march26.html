THIS WEEK'S FEATURE: JIWON

Hello! I am Jiwon. I am a senior classical voice student from Manhattan School of Music. I am trying hard to sing well!

FUN FACT

If I have spare time, I draw. I always draw to give to someone that I really appreciate and love. The result always comes out with as much effort as I put in! So I become like a zombie until I finish them. No sleep and no happiness until the end. A real zombie. However, it relieves my stress. As I draw, I can only think of the person that I sincerely love. Not about any other things, but love and care. Despite getting tired physically, I never get tired spiritually.
